Our Services

Discover clarity and precision with our expert optometry services

Eye Exams

Adult - $80: Our comprehensive eye exam for adults includes a thorough assessment of vision and eye health. We use advanced technology to evaluate visual acuity, detect refractive errors, and screen for common eye diseases such as glaucoma and macular degeneration. With personalized care and detailed analysis, we ensure optimal vision and eye health for adults, empowering you to see the world clearly and confidently tackle your daily tasks.

Children - $60: Our pediatric comprehensive eye exam is designed specifically for children. We use child-friendly techniques to assess vision, eye muscle coordination, and eye health. Our compassionate approach helps ease any apprehensions your child may have, fostering a positive experience during their eye exam. By detecting and addressing any vision issues early, we support your child's learning and development, ensuring they can explore the world with confidence and enthusiasm.
Contact Lens Fitting

Adult - $100: Our contact lens fitting service for adults ensures comfortable and clear vision with contact lenses. We conduct a comprehensive evaluation of your eye structure, tear film, and lifestyle factors to recommend the most suitable lenses for your needs. From daily disposables to specialized lenses for astigmatism or multifocal prescriptions, we provide expert guidance to meet your vision requirements, allowing you to enjoy every moment with clarity and comfort, whether at work or play.

Child - $90: Our pediatric contact lens fitting service is tailored to the unique needs of children and adolescents. We prioritize comfort, safety, and visual clarity when recommending contact lenses for young wearers. Our experienced optometrists work closely with children and their parents to ensure proper lens selection and fitting, while also educating them on proper lens care and hygiene. By offering a range of contact lens options suitable for active lifestyles, we empower young individuals to engage in activities with freedom and confidence, without the hindrance of glasses.
Digital Eye Strain Evaluation

Adult - $50: Our digital eye strain evaluation for adults addresses the visual discomfort associated with prolonged screen time. We conduct specialized tests to assess factors such as screen brightness, viewing distance, and blinking frequency, providing personalized recommendations to alleviate symptoms such as headaches, dry eyes, and blurred vision. Our holistic approach considers lifestyle factors and ergonomic adjustments to improve visual comfort and productivity in today's digital world, ensuring you can stay focused and connected without the discomfort of eye strain.

Children - $40: Our pediatric digital eye strain evaluation focuses on identifying and addressing the impact of digital device usage on children's vision. We offer practical strategies and lifestyle adjustments to minimize eye strain and promote healthy screen habits from a young age. Through interactive discussions and age-appropriate guidance, we empower both children and parents to implement effective strategies for reducing digital eye strain, supporting children's overall well-being and academic success, while also nurturing a healthy relationship with technology.
LASIK Surgery Services

Adult - $2000 per eye: Our LASIK surgery for adults offers a life-changing solution for vision correction. With advanced laser technology and experienced surgeons, we provide personalized LASIK procedures tailored to your unique vision needs. Say goodbye to glasses and contacts, and hello to clear, crisp vision without the hassle. Our comprehensive pre-surgery consultation ensures you are a suitable candidate, while our post-surgery care guarantees a smooth recovery process, allowing you to enjoy the benefits of improved vision quickly and safely.

Child - Not Applicable: LASIK surgery is typically not recommended for children. However, we offer comprehensive pediatric eye exams and other vision correction options such as prescription glasses and contact lenses to address vision issues in children and adolescents. Our focus is on ensuring healthy eye development and providing solutions that support their growth and daily activities.
Eyewear Collection

Adults - Frames starting from $100: Explore our extensive selection of designer frames for adults, offering style and functionality to suit your individual taste and vision requirements. Whether you're looking for trendy, sophisticated, or classic eyewear, we have options to complement your personal style and enhance your visual comfort. Our experienced opticians assist you in finding the perfect fit and style, ensuring your eyewear not only improves your vision but also enhances your overall look. With brands like Ray-Ban, Oakley, and Gucci, you can be assured of high-quality and fashionable choices.

Children - Frames starting from $80: Our children's eyewear collection combines durability, comfort, and style to meet the unique needs of young wearers. With a variety of colors, shapes, and sizes, we make choosing eyewear fun and exciting for children, while ensuring durability and safety for their active lifestyles. Our kid-friendly frames are designed to withstand the rigors of daily use while providing the necessary vision correction. We also offer a range of sports eyewear options to protect your child’s eyes during athletic activities, ensuring their vision remains sharp and protected at all times.

Our Experts

Meet our dedicated team of experienced optometrists

Emily Hayes

Dr. Hayes specializes in pediatric optometry, ensuring the youngest patients receive thorough eye care tailored to their unique needs. With a gentle and compassionate approach, she strives to make every child's visit to the optometrist a positive experience.

Michael Chen

Dr. Chen is an expert in contact lens fittings and management of ocular diseases such as glaucoma and diabetic retinopathy. He is dedicated to staying at the forefront of advancements in optometry to provide his patients with the best possible care and vision correction.

Sarah Green

With a focus on geriatric optometry, Dr. Green is committed to helping older adults maintain their vision and eye health as they age. She specializes in conditions such as cataracts and age-related macular degeneration, providing personalized treatment plans for enhancement.

Jenny Brown

Dr. Brown is passionate about sports vision and enhancing athletic performance through specialized vision training and corrective lenses. She works closely with athletes of all levels, from weekend warriors to professional competitors, to optimize their visual skills.

Rachel Kim

Dr. Kim specializes in low vision rehabilitation, helping patients with visual impairments adapt to their condition and regain independence in their daily lives. She offers comprehensive assessments and customized assistive devices to improve her patients' ability to perform tasks such as reading, writing, and navigation.

Lexie Lee

As an expert in refractive surgery co-management, Dr. Lee collaborates closely with ophthalmologists to ensure seamless pre- and post-operative care for patients undergoing procedures such as LASIK and cataract surgery. She provides thorough evaluations and counseling to help patients achieve their desired vision correction outcomes.

Daniel Smith

With a focus on community outreach and education, Dr. Smith is dedicated to promoting eye health and prevention of vision problems through public seminars, school screenings, and volunteer initiatives. He is committed to empowering individuals and families to prioritize their eye care and maintain lifelong vision wellness.

Alexandra Russo

Dr. Russo specializes in specialty contact lenses for conditions such as keratoconus, irregular corneas, and post-surgical complications. She has extensive experience fitting custom lenses to provide comfortable and effective vision correction for patients with complex eye conditions, improving their quality of life and visual function.
